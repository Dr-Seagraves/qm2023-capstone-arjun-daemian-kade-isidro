There are periods where the CPI and foreign investment correlate well, such as between 2017 and 2020, where they both see minor declines, as well as after 2022 when they both experience sharp declines. 2016 and 2021 are both years where the relationship breaks down, with 2016 seeing growth on one and a decline on the other, and 2021 having a sharp increase in investment as average CPI stays the same.
